Apply to Detroit Automation Academy

Join our 12-week hands-on curriculum in robotics, CAD design, and autonomous systems.

üéì Student Application Process

Program Overview

Duration: 12 weeks (March 8 - May 24, 2026)

Schedule: Tuesdays and Thursdays, 4:30 PM - 7:00 PM

Location: Boys & Girls Club of Greater Detroit & Michigan Central

Cost: FREE for all students from Detroit

Prerequisites: Enthusiasm to learn! No prior experience necessary.

What You'll Learn

  • Phase 1: Electronics & GPIO - Control hardware with Python, build event-driven applications
  • Phase 2: CAD & Fabrication - Design 3D models, laser cut parts, 3D print prototypes
  • Phase 3: Autonomous Systems - Program rover navigation, implement algorithms, test in real environments
  • Real Hardware - Work with Raspberry Pi, motors, sensors, and custom-designed robots
  • Team Projects - Collaborate with peers on real-world automation challenges
  • Portfolio Building - Create projects for your resume and college applications

How to Apply

  1. Fill out the online form (click button below) - takes 5 minutes
  2. Share a bit about yourself - what interests you about automation?
  3. Get approval from a parent/guardian - we need their consent
  4. Attend student orientation - March 5, 2026 (5 PM - 6 PM)
  5. Start the program! - March 8, 2026

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need programming experience?

No! We teach Python from the ground up. The only requirement is enthusiasm and a willingness to learn. Many of our students have never coded before.

What if I can't make every Saturday?

We understand life happens. We require attendance at 10+ of 12 sessions. Talk to us if you have scheduling conflicts!

Do I need to buy anything?

Nope! Everything is provided:

  • Raspberry Pi boards & sensors
  • Laptop access (we have spares)
  • 3D printer & laser cutter use
  • Snacks & lunch (we'll have you covered!)

What happens after the 12 weeks?

You'll have completed a full automation curriculum and built a portfolio of projects. We'll help you transition to advanced cohorts, internships, or college programs. Plus, you'll be part of our alumni network!

Is this for college prep or just fun?

Both! Our curriculum aligns with manufacturing and tech industry certifications. Many students use this for college admissions portfolios and internship applications. But it's also genuinely fun hands-on learning.

What's the time commitment?

Sessions are Saturdays 10 AM - 1 PM (3 hours). Plus 1-2 hours of optional homework per week if you want to dive deeper. You set your own pace!

Can I bring a friend?

Absolutely! We love when students refer friends. Applying together is encouraged‚Äîyou'll have built-in study partners for the 12 weeks.
