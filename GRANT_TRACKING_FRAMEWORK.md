## üéØ GRANT WRITING BEST PRACTICES

### 1. Funder Alignment (Critical)
- ‚úÖ Use their language & terms
- ‚úÖ Connect to their stated priorities
- ‚úÖ Show you understand their mission
- ‚úÖ Cite their recent grant patterns
- ‚úÖ Meet all requirements exactly

### 2. Compelling Narrative
- ‚úÖ Start with a story (not statistics)
- ‚úÖ Show urgency & clear problem
- ‚úÖ Demonstrate why NOW is important
- ‚úÖ Build credibility with evidence
- ‚úÖ Make impact specific & measurable

### 3. Clarity & Brevity
- ‚úÖ Use short sentences & paragraphs
- ‚úÖ Avoid jargon or explain all terms
- ‚úÖ Use active voice & concrete examples
- ‚úÖ Support claims with data
- ‚úÖ Never exceed page limits

### 4. Realistic Budget
- ‚úÖ Budget aligns with project scope
- ‚úÖ Includes all necessary costs
- ‚úÖ Justifies every expense
- ‚úÖ Shows financial sustainability
- ‚úÖ No obvious padding or waste

### 5. Measurable Outcomes
- ‚úÖ Goals are SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound)
- ‚úÖ Metrics are tracked systematically
- ‚úÖ Evaluation plan is clear & realistic
- ‚úÖ Benchmarks show impact
- ‚úÖ Defines success criteria
