## ðŸŽ¯ Quick Start for Events

### For Event Coordinators:
1. Open `event_checklists/EVENT_CHECKLIST_FIXED.md`
2. Review pre-event checklist at least 1 week before event
3. Assign staff roles and verify equipment status
4. Brief all student assistants on their zone scripts

### For Student Assistants:
1. Choose your zone: Design Lab (Zone 1), Rapid Prototyping (Zone 2), or Autonomous Systems (Zone 3)
2. Read the corresponding zone script thoroughly (at least 2 times)
3. Practice the script during Feb 2 rehearsal
4. Arrive 15 minutes early for setup on event days
5. Follow the script closely, but add your own personality!

### For Zone Leads/Technicians:
1. Review your zone script AND the event checklist
2. Pre-test all equipment (laptops, laser cutter, rovers) the day before
3. Set up safety procedures and backup equipment
4. Be ready to troubleshoot issues quickly

## âš ï¸ Critical Safety Notes

### Zone 2: Laser Cutting
- **NEVER** run the laser with the enclosure door open
- **NEVER** leave the laser unattended while running
- Always verify the fire extinguisher is accessible
- Ensure ventilation fan is running at all times
- Keep guests at least 3 feet away from the laser cutter

### Zone 3: Robotics
- Test all rovers before each demo session
- Keep backup rover powered and ready
- Do not let guests operate rovers without training

## ðŸ”§ Equipment Setup Timelines

### Day Before Event (Feb 2):
- Test all laptops and projector (Zone 1)
- Test laser cutter and load G-code files (Zone 2)
- Test both rovers and sensors (Zone 3)
- Verify WiFi connectivity
- Charge all batteries to 100%

### 2 Hours Before Event:
- Deploy equipment to each zone
- Run one full demo at each zone
- Brief all staff and student assistants
- Verify emergency contacts are posted
- Position safety equipment (fire extinguishers, first aid)

### During Event:
- Hourly equipment checks
- Monitor guest flow between zones
- Troubleshoot issues quickly
- Capture media/photos
- Track attendance and interest forms
